About

This website is a collection of AI-assisted math resources designed for use in middle school classrooms. The projects include interactive games, visual learning tools, review activities, worksheets, and practice applications aligned to Massachusetts mathematics standards.

Resources are organized by grade level, standard, and type to help teachers quickly find activities that fit their lessons. Many of the tools were created to increase student engagement through game-based learning, visual models, and interactive exploration of mathematical concepts.

New projects and resources are added regularly.
